Welcome to Cipher

Cipher turns architect-specified roofing products into Soprema substitution requests in under two minutes. Here's the four-step flow:

  1. 01
    Upload
    Drop a spec PDF (the project's full specification book or just the roofing section).
  2. 02
    Analyze
    Cipher reads the spec, finds the architect-specified roofing products, and matches them to Soprema's catalog.
  3. 03
    Review & generate
    Confirm the matches, fill in the project context (architect, location, etc.), and Cipher drafts the substitution request.
  4. 04
    Export
    One-click "Download full package" — cover letter + property comparison + every Soprema datasheet, merged into a single bookmarked PDF ready to email.

First time? — no upload needed.
